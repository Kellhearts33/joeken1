About Dr. Joel & Joeken Chemist

With over 6 years of experience in pharmaceutical sciences and clinical practice, Dr. Joel established Joeken Chemist with a vision to provide comprehensive healthcare services that prioritize patient safety, medication efficacy, and personalized care.

Our state-of-the-art facility in Eldoret combines traditional pharmaceutical expertise with modern healthcare technology to deliver exceptional medical services. We believe in building lasting relationships with our patients through trust, transparency, and clinical excellence.

At Joeken Chemist, we are committed to being your reliable healthcare partner, offering not just medications but complete wellness solutions that improve your quality of life and promote optimal health outcomes for you and your family.
